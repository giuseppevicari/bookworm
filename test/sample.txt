Chapter 1

It was the best of times, it was the worst of times, it was the age of wisdom,
it was the age of foolishness, it was the epoch of belief, it was the epoch of
incredulity, it was the season of Light, it was the season of Darkness.

Darcy stood at the window. Elizabeth watched him carefully. The rain fell softly
on the garden below. Darcy turned and their eyes met. Elizabeth looked away.

Chapter 2

Many months had passed. Elizabeth had not forgotten Darcy, nor had Darcy forgotten
Elizabeth. Their paths crossed again at Netherfield, where Bingley had returned.
Jane smiled warmly at Bingley. Elizabeth observed Darcy with quiet curiosity.

Chapter 3

The letter arrived on a Tuesday. Elizabeth read it twice, then a third time.
Darcy had written with characteristic brevity. Yet every word carried weight.
Elizabeth sat down heavily. The room felt suddenly smaller. Jane entered and
saw her sister's pale expression. What has happened, she asked.
