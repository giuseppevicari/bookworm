The Lord of the Rings Test

Chapter I

The Nazgûl were the Ringwraiths, servants of the Dark Lord Sauron. They were nine in number. Each nazgul had once been a king of men. The Nazgûl hunted Frodo across the Shire. When the nazgul cried out, all hearts were filled with dread. The Black Riders, also known as Nazgûl, rode black horses. Frodo feared the nazgul more than anything else. The Nazgûl were nearly invisible in the daylight.

Chapter II

Gandalf spoke of the nazgul to Frodo. The Nazgûl would stop at nothing to recover the Ring.
